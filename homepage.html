Carpentry is the process of shaping Timber, using hand tools. The Products produced are used in buliding construction, such as doors and windows, furniture manufacturing. Carpentry work mainly involves the joining together of wooden work pieces and finishing the surfaces after shaping them. Hence, the term joining is also used commonly for carpentry.
